Our School

Shree Vidya Jyoti Secondary School is a hub of academic excellence located in Sundarbazar-5-khahare lamjung - Nepal. With over two decades of educational legacy, the school offers a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to grow intellectually and morally. Our experienced teachers, innovative teaching methods, and modern facilities ensure holistic development for each learner. We aim to build responsible citizens with strong values and creative thinking.

Science Lab
A school science lab is a place where students explore scientific concepts through experiments and practical activities. It is equipped with tools, chemicals, and safety equipment that help students understand biology, physics, and chemistry. The lab encourages curiosity, critical thinking, and hands-on learning, making science more interesting and effective in developing real-world understanding. .
Computer Lab
A school computer lab is a room equipped with computers and internet access where students learn digital skills. It helps them practice programming, typing, research, and using educational software. The lab supports subjects like ICT and coding, and promotes technological literacy. It also prepares students for the modern digital world through hands-on computer-based learning.
Sports Ground
School sports and grounds are essential for students’ physical and mental development. They provide space for games like football, cricket, volleyball, and running. Regular sports activities promote fitness, teamwork, discipline, and leadership skills. Well-maintained playgrounds encourage active lifestyles and help students balance studies with physical well-being, making school life more enjoyable and healthy.
